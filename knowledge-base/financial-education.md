## üí∞ Personal Finance Fundamentals

### Budgeting Basics
**50/30/20 Rule**:
- 50% - Needs (housing, utilities, groceries)
- 30% - Wants (entertainment, dining out)
- 20% - Savings and debt repayment

**Zero-Based Budgeting**:
- Assign every dollar a purpose
- Income minus expenses equals zero
- Prioritize essential expenses first

**Envelope Method**:
- Allocate cash for spending categories
- Use physical or digital envelopes
- Stop spending when envelope is empty

### Emergency Fund Guidelines
- **Starter Fund**: $1,000 minimum
- **Full Fund**: 3-6 months of expenses
- **High-Risk Jobs**: 6-12 months recommended
- **Keep in**: High-yield savings account

## üìà Investment Education

### Investment Types
**Stocks**: 
- Ownership shares in companies
- Potential for high returns and high risk
- Long-term growth strategy

**Bonds**:
- Loans to companies or governments
- Generally lower risk than stocks
- Steady income through interest

**Mutual Funds**:
- Professionally managed portfolios
- Diversification across multiple investments
- Various risk levels available

**ETFs (Exchange-Traded Funds)**:
- Trade like stocks on exchanges
- Lower fees than mutual funds
- Instant diversification

### Risk Assessment Levels
**Conservative (Low Risk)**:
- 70% bonds, 30% stocks
- Suitable for near-retirement
- Lower returns, more stability

**Moderate (Medium Risk)**:
- 60% stocks, 40% bonds
- Balanced growth and income
- Good for medium-term goals

**Aggressive (High Risk)**:
- 80-90% stocks, 10-20% bonds
- Maximum growth potential
- Suitable for long-term investors

## üìä Financial Ratios and Metrics

### Debt-to-Income Ratio
- **Formula**: Total monthly debt payments / Monthly income
- **Ideal**: Below 36% (including mortgage)
- **Maximum**: 43% for most loan approvals

### Savings Rate
- **Formula**: Monthly savings / Monthly income
- **Recommended**: 20% or higher
- **Minimum**: 10% for retirement

### Net Worth Calculation
- **Formula**: Assets - Liabilities = Net Worth
- **Track**: Monthly or quarterly
- **Goal**: Positive and growing

### Home Buying
**Down Payment**: 10-20% of home value
**Closing Costs**: 2-5% of purchase price
**Monthly Payment Rule**: No more than 28% of income

### Car Buying
**Down Payment**: 10-20% recommended
**Loan Term**: 4-6 years maximum
**Total Transportation**: No more than 20% of income
